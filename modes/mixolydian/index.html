Mixolydian

Bluesy, dominant, rock

About Mixolydian

Bluesy, dominant, rock. Used in blues, rock, country, funk.

Famous examples: Norwegian Wood (Beatles), Sweet Home Chicago, Gimme Shelter.

Characteristic note: the ♭7 (flat seventh) gives it a bluesy, dominant feel.
