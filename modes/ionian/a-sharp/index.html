HAPPY, BRIGHT, RESOLVED

A♯ Ionian is a major mode with a happy, bright, resolved character. It is the I mode of A♯ major — it uses the same 7 notes as A♯ Ionian but is rooted on A♯, which shifts the harmonic focus and creates a completely different sound. The tonic chord is A♯maj7.

Where to use A♯ Ionian

Major key melodies, pop, folk, classical.

Famous examples

  • Let It Be
  • most pop songs
