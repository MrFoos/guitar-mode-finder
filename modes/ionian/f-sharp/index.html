HAPPY, BRIGHT, RESOLVED

F♯ Ionian is a major mode with a happy, bright, resolved character. It is the I mode of F♯ major — it uses the same 7 notes as F♯ Ionian but is rooted on F♯, which shifts the harmonic focus and creates a completely different sound. The tonic chord is F♯maj7.

Where to use F♯ Ionian

Major key melodies, pop, folk, classical.
