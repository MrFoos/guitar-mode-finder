Phrygian

About Phrygian

Dark, tense, Spanish/flamenco. Used in metal, flamenco, film scores.

Famous examples: White Zombie riffs, flamenco progressions.

Characteristic note: the ♭2 (flat second) creates its dark, Spanish-sounding tension.
