Dorian

About Dorian

Soulful, minor but with hope. Used in jazz, blues-rock, funk, latin.

Famous examples: So What (Miles Davis), Oye Como Va, Scarborough Fair.

Characteristic note: the natural 6th separates Dorian from the minor (Aeolian).
