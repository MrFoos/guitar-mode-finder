Lydian

Dreamy, floating, ethereal
CHARACTER · DREAMY, FLOATING, ETHEREAL

Dreamy, floating, ethereal. Used in film music, jazz, progressive rock.

Famous examples: The Simpsons theme, Flying (Beatles), Lydian film scores.

Characteristic note: the ♯4 (raised fourth) gives it a dreamy, floating quality.
