Aeolian

Melancholic, natural minor

About Aeolian

Melancholic, natural minor. Used in rock, pop, classical, metal.

Famous examples: Stairway to Heaven, Hotel California, Sultans of Swing.

Characteristic note: the ♭6 (flat sixth) deepens its melancholic minor character.
