Locrian

About Locrian

Unstable, tense, dissonant. Used in metal, jazz, used sparingly.

Famous examples: Half-diminished chord contexts, Metallica solos.

Characteristic note: the ♭5 (diminished fifth) makes it unstable and rarely used as a tonal center.
